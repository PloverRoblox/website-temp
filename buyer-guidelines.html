Buyer and License Holder Guidelines

Last Updated Sunday, June 20th

Getting a License

When getting a license, the group owner should be the one holding it. Developers cannot use their own licenses individually and must transfer ownership to the group owner. If we find that groups are not following this policy, Plover reserves the right to blacklist you and the institution you represent from registering new licenses, joining the Discord, and joining our group. We can also file a DMCA takedown in some cases.

Hiring Devs and Game Access

When buying a license, Plover contributors will ask for a list of people with access to your place. We require that you consult Plover when hiring more developers. This process helps us keep track of who is in contact with Plover technology. It can also be a great way to background check developers. Failure to follow this rule gives Plover the right to blacklist you and the institution you represent from registering new licenses, joining the Discord, and joining our group. We can also file a DMCA claim in some cases.

Leaking via Place Stealing or Untrusted Developer

If a game you own has been leaked, we require you start a ticket in Plover immediately. List all items that were leaked and send a link to the leaked place. Plover staff will handle DMCA claiming, which will grant us the opportunity to revoke any tech products.

Leaking via The Buyer

If a buyer is found leaking a game with Plover tech, or the actual models, the buyer will be blacklisted from Plover with no appeal. All assets they own will be revoked and they will be banned from all Plover Discord Servers.

Asking for Models

If you are found asking for free Plover assets, Plover staff have the right to blacklist you and the institution you represent from registering new licenses, joining the Discord, and joining our group.

What it Means For The Buyer When Plover Tech is Leaked

Buyers of certain Plover products will be notified via announcement. The model that has been leaked will have its mesh revoked and buyers will be forced to replace them. Buyers will be given a replacement model in place of the leaked model, free of charge. This ensures that Plover can maintain low prices and prevent sale shutdowns.

Recoloring and Rebranding

Recoloring of Plover tech is allowed if logos and branding stay visible. For example if you make a bed Black, you should make the Plover logo white. There must be a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 between the background and the logo. Rebranding Plover tech is a huge violation and can lead to a blacklist and a DMCA takedown.

Reselling and Trading

Reselling Plover tech is prohibited. Redistribution of tech licenses is also prohibited, buyers found reselling or trading Plover tech will be blacklisted immediately with no appeal. Plover will also file a DMCA claim against the buyers game and group.

Selling Pre-Built Hospitals

Buyers are not allowed to purchase Plover licenses for the sole purpose of tech being placed into hospital games for sale. When selling buildings you have created, you are required to remove all Plover tech from them. Selling pre-built hospitals containing Plover Tech is equal to independently reselling it. This can lead to the same punishments found in the Reselling and Trading section.

Selling Groups with Plover Technology in their Games

Selling groups is against the Roblox Terms of Service. Plover dislikes entities or individuals who mass purchase hospitals form associations. When selling your group, please inform the buyer that they must also pay for the Plover licensing. Moreover, this also means that the buyer is not blacklisted from Plover. If the seller sees fit, they may also transfer over all their licenses to the buyer by opening a ticket in our main Discord. However, all group selling license transfer are final and will not be reversed. Plover will not assist with scamming in these cases.

Investigation Notice

When you use the tech you got from an ingenuine source, or you are reported for having leaked tech, you give Plover the right to deploy contributors to take note of every item in your campus and report back to Plover. You are required to have the buyer certification notice you received during your purchase of the equipment in your hospital lobby or visible behind a reception desk If you do not, Plover will assume the medical equipment you have is leaked and launch an investigation.

Punishments

Plover reserves the right to do the following if you do not follow the rules in this agreement.

  • Blacklist you and any institutions you represent.
  • Revoke Plover Plus benefits you have paid for.
  • Revoke tech licenses or refuse to register more tech to you or any institutions you represent.
  • Deny support to you, mute you in any Discord servers Plover owns.
  • Ban you from all Discord servers Plover owns.
  • Kick you from any Discord servers Plover owns.
  • Ban you from any games Plover owns.
  • Mute you in any games in Plover owns.
  • Report you to Plover partners so they may punish you in accordance to their policies.
  • File a DMCA takedown on your group and games associated with it.
