Discord Server Guidelines

Guidelines for Plover and Plover Legal.

Do not organize, participate in, or encourage harassment of others. Disagreements happen and are normal, but continuous, repetitive, or severe negative comments may cross the line into harassment and are not okay.

Do not talk badly about others in chatrooms. It’s unacceptable to attack a person or a community based on attributes such as their race, ethnicity, national origin, sex, gender, sexual orientation, religious affiliation, or disabilities.

Do not make threats of violence or threaten to harm others. This includes indirect threats, as well as sharing or threatening to share someone’s private personal information (also known as doxxing).

Do not evade user blocks or server bans. Do not send unwanted, repeated friend requests or messages, especially after they’ve made it clear they don’t want to talk to you anymore. Do not try to hide your identity in an attempt to contact someone who has blocked you, or otherwise circumvent the tools we have which enable users to protect themselves.

Do not send others viruses or malware, attempt to phish others, hack them or DDoS them.

You may not sexualize minors in any way. This includes sharing content or links which depict minors in a pornographic, sexually suggestive, or violent manner, and includes illustrated or digitally altered pornography that depicts minors (such as lolicon, shotacon, or cub). We report illegal content to Discord.

You may not share sexually explicit content of other people without their consent, or share or promote sharing of non- consensual intimate imagery (also known as revenge porn) in an attempt to shame or degrade someone.

You may not share content that glorifies or promotes suicide or self-harm, including any encouragement to others to cut themselves, or embrace eating disorders such as anorexia or bulimia.

You may not share images of sadistic gore or animal cruelty.

Do not mess up counting in #counting on purpose. This could include messing up with random numbers multiple times.

Do not Advertise in the Discord server. This includes advertising in DMs and tickets. Advertising in the Marketplace category is allowed as long as the specific rules for it are followed.

Do not spam ping roles/users.

Do not ask for free tech or advertise yourself giving away free tech.

Do not try to bypass our filters with slurs like the N**ger, Tra*ny, and Fag*ot. Any term that may be considered derogatory to another group is also not allowed.

Do not misuse Plover marketplace and follow all rules in it.

If you are a buyer, you must follow the buyer agreement.

Do not leak Plover assets. This includes leaking in direct messages, server chat channels, tickets, and subsidiary servers. This also includes files and links that contain Plover assets.

Do not join the Plover Discord if you are under 13 years old. Staff members may challenge you to verify your identity via student identification card without your last name, school, and other identifiable information present if reported.

Follow the Discord and Roblox community guidelines. Any violations will get you banned and will be reported to both companies.

Do not spread leaked games or assets in Plover chatrooms or in Direct Messages with other users. This can be from any group on Roblox. Plover understands how hard it is to have assets leaked and wants to help combat that.

Alternative Accounts will be banned. Alternative accounts can become a security threat and can be considered ban evasion. When searching for alternative accounts (alts) we look for inactivity, not being verified with Bloxlink, not accepting server agreements, Nitro and Hypesquad status, Roblox account data, account creation date, chat history in other Discord servers and with users we ask.

General Plover Guidelines

Guidelines for Everything Plover.

Do not buy Plover tech from anywhere other than Plover. You can be fined or banned for buying tech anywhere other than the Plover Discord or any product hubs.

Do not use cheats to obtain Plover tech. Using cheats to obtain items you would normally buy is looked down upon and not allowed.

Do not attempt to break into staff emails. We can track IP addresses.

Do not attempt a Denial of Service attacks, or any malicious attack that may break the Computer Abuse and Fraud Act on any Plover assets, domains, etc. If you do you will be reported to the proper authorities.

Do not modify software / mesh identification to create a new untraceable / cracked product.

Do not use exploits in Plover SMP or raids spawn areas. This includes using any client modifications such as wrust unless the use is for shaders.

Do not use exploits in Plover owned games. This includes any scripts that must be injected into the Roblox client using Synapse X, Calamari, Mint, SirHut, and any other executors. Also note client modifications are not allowed.

Do not attempt to attempt to overthrow Plover or any subsidiaries it owns.
